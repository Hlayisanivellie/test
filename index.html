A new journey lies ahead of us
THE GOLD FIELDS Way

At its heart, culture is the glue that binds a company together. While we might all be different, we have our shared common Gold Fields culture. This is the Gold Fields Way.

We are one inclusive team – caring, connected and courageous – delivering great impact together

AMBITION

This is our ambition – the culture we aspire to have. This is what makes us #StrongerTogether

One
We are part of One Gold Fields – a united team with a single, unifying Purpose, Vision, set of Values and culture. While we recognise and celebrate the many different people, languages, countries and cultures we have around the world, we know that we are all part of one family that has one purpose and one common Gold Fields culture.
Caring
We want people to feel they truly matter – something that can only be achieved through a culture of deep care, respect and kindness. We want to build a company where people are eager and happy to come to work, where they can build positive relationships with their colleagues and can experience a sense of emotional and psychological safety.
Courageous
We can achieve great things together if we have the courage to dream big and to push our personal and business boundaries. We look for new and better ways of doing things, for innovative ways to get the job done safely and smartly. We are also brave enough to speak up, have difficult conversations and listen to challenging feedback.
Inclusive team
We know that our team is stronger when we include people with different talents, world views, cultural contexts, and problem-solving approaches. Diversity, equity and inclusion  makes us stronger, faster, smarter, better, and inclusivity creates a space where everyone feels they belong.
Connected
Being connected means working together towards a common goal, with a single purpose in mind. While we operate in locations across the globe and in many different disciplines and departments, being connected pulls us together as a team and enables us to work smarter and seamlessly together.
Delivering great impact together
When we work together we can make a positive impact on the lives of all the people touched by our business. This is what it means to be #StrongerTogether

THEMES
Four priority themes will
bring our ambition to life.
1
ONE TEAM
One caring, inclusive, empowered team
We belong to one Gold Fields team. We have common values and one shared identity. We work together, caring, supporting and trusting each other. We delegate and empower, respect individual voices and listen to one another to be collectively powerful.
2
RESPECTFUL WORKPLACE
Guaranteed human dignity
Together we create a safe working environment where we all feel respected and valued. We are truly empowered to speak up in an environment free of harmful behaviour.
3
WORKING SMARTER TOGETHER
Efficient, agile and tech-enabled
Together we are ordered, efficient and agile. We collaborate with ease across teams, functions and regions to achieve the right momentum. Smart processes and technology, supported by data and insights, enable working smarter together. We anticipate and adopt technology to enable better ways of working. We deliver great impact with ease.
4
UNLOCKING POTENTIAL
Learning, innovation and aspiration
We are courageous and ambitious for ourselves and our organisation. We are forward-looking, continuously seeking new challenges and embracing learning to unlock our shared Purpose and Vision. Our working environment provides opportunities to be curious, innovate and share best practice. We are a learning organisation invested in building capability and offering inspiring career journeys.
ATTRIBUTES
Our culture attributes will bring our culture to life. These behaviours make it a living reality.

SAFETY

We take care of each other every day
We each take personal responsibility to ensure we are safe and healthy at work every day. Gold Fields is a safe place to work.

FAIR

Our culture is built on fairness and trust
Our ethics and values are lived daily, we embrace diversity, equity and inclusion. We act fairly in everything we do.

PARTNERING

We actively engage and collaborate with our business partners and stakeholders to build value-adding and trusted relationships
We are a trusted and valued mining partner; we build strong relationships with our business partners and stakeholders, based on trust, honesty and frequent engagement.

COLLABORATIVE

We deliver together, connecting seamlessly across our business
We align and work together smoothly across teams, functions and regions to deliver the best possible outcomes.

RESULTS-FOCUSED

We focus on achieving superior results together. In pursuit of excellence, we deliver quality work and tenaciously deliver on our commitments.

CARING

We have a culture of deep care, respect and empathy
Care runs deep in our culture. People truly feel they matter. With respect and empathy, we build positive working relationships and psychological safety. People are eager and happy to come to work.

EMPOWERED

Our culture of participation and ownership allows us to thrive together
Our people are truly empowered to speak up and act with individual autonomy; management encourages and listens to ideas from everyone. We take shared ownership for our future success.

EFFICIENCY

Efficiency is fueled by our agility, smart processes and technology
Our people, processes and technology work effectively together. We respond nimbly to change and pivot as needed. We are resilient in the face of challenges and setbacks.

LEARNING

Learning and innovation are at the heart of our culture
With a learning mindset we are adaptive and open, we embrace digital innovation, encourage creativity and stay open to new and different ideas in the spirit of continuous improvement.

VISIONARY

We are a forward-looking, courageous and inspiring place to work. We are strategic and purposeful in our thinking. We are ambitious and courageous in making the decisions to shape our future.

Wherever we are in the world, whatever language we speak, whatever country or mine we work in, we are united by a common Gold Fields Way.

This is what makes us
